About Our Project

This website is designed to scrape and monitor job vacancies, providing an easy and efficient way to keep track of available opportunities. The idea for this project was conceived by Dereck Lebon and Sarvesh Jowata, who saw a need for a streamlined approach to job searching. The Recruiting module on Dayforce, the application both are working on in their current job, has inspired them a lot.
